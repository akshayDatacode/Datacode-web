About Datacode

Datacode is a technical programming and e-learning community dedicated to expand the coding knowledge among the coding enthusiasts. Our vision is that every person who is interested in coding should have the opportunity to learn. We organize contests and daily quizzes to encourage the learners. Datacode also organizes the weekly competitions which has students engaged from all over World. Datacode increases diversity by reaching learners of all backgrounds and inspire them to keep learning.
